Fitness and Diet

I'm developing a comprehensive website for fitness and diet enthusiasts. The platform offers a personalized two-week program featuring three diet meals and three exercises each day, tailored to the user's weight, height, gender, and intensity level. Users can track their daily progress, access detailed exercise instructions, and discover nutritious recipes. The goal is to provide a holistic approach to fitness and nutrition, ensuring users stay motivated and achieve their health goals. The website also includes reminders to stay hydrated, enhancing the overall wellness experience.
